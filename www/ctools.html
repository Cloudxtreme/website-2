Installation

  • • Click on the link and copy the command.

  • • In Minecraft™, type /give @p minecraft:command_block

  • • Right click on the command block and paste in the command

  • • On the right, click on Needs Redstone It should now say Always Active

  • • Click Done and the contraption should now appear
